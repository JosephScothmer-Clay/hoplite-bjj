About Us

Hoplite BJJ is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu academy in Southampton focused on discipline, respect, and growth. We welcome students of all levels to join our friendly, supportive team and improve at their own pace in a safe, positive environment.

Our Values

  • Be Humble — Respect everyone regardless of rank and train safely, without ego.
  • Be Honest — Communicate openly about what you need and how you learn best.
  • Be Respectful — Bigotry and discrimination of any kind have no place here.
  • Be Considerate — Look out for your training partners and help keep everyone safe.

Meet Our Head Coach

Adam Wilson – Head Coach

Known as “Grizzly Adam”, Adam brings years of experience and a passion for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Starting with Kung Fu before transitioning fully to BJJ, he has competed across the UK and internationally, including the Paris Open.

His style combines relentless top pressure with creative submissions like mounted triangles and wrist locks — “because of how mad they make people.”

Adam offers private sessions for personal coaching and fast progress. DM him on Instagram to book: @grizzlyadambjj or contact us here to schedule a private session.
